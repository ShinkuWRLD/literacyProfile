Challenges

Life can often become overwhelming and chaotic once factors out of our control begin to occur. I've been fortunate enough to have a strong support system (via my family and friends) to help me through the tough times I did face. I've been also fortunate enough to be equipped to face these challenges, since my life challenges could've been much worse. I've used these experiences primarily to help myself grow in the writing sphere and gain a stronger sense of social awareness. Last semester, I took Honors class 115 which tied into the ideas of identity and sense of belonging. I used my own experiences to connect with the authors that we studied and gain more insight on certain social issues and challenges that have existed and still exist to this day.
