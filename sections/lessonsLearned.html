Lessons Learned

Everyone has their own unique experiences and lessons learned from them. I had (and still have) a somewhat stubborn approach to life and I like to do things my way and by myself. I am currently learning the detriments to that approach and that myopic mindset prevents learning and the betterment of myself. I'm also learning the importance of trusting and relying on others which helps me understand and utilize other people's ideas and perspectives. If I continue to do things in soley in my particular way, then I cannot garner any useful insight that would be beneficial to not only me as a writer but as a person. Although, typically for writing assignments in school its hard to display and incorporate these insights without the risk of losing points or "getting a bad grade". Because of this, I have also learned how to write formulaicly and try to get the best way possible on assignments, such as essays on texts and supporting arguments that I don't believe in for those aforementioned texts. These lessons I've learned are mainly tied into my life experiences as any other person's are and its important to not minimize the experiences and perspectives of others.
